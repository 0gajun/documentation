## Add a PagerDuty service

Add a PagerDuty service to the Datadog integration.

**ARGUMENTS**:

* **`service_name`** [*required*]: Your Service name in PagerDuty.

* **`service_key`** [*required*]: Your Service name associated service key in Pagerduty.

## Access

Admin users can access the [Usage][1] page by hovering over their username at the bottom left then navigate to:  
`Plan & Usage`--> `Usage`.

## Available data

The Usage details page provides the following:

* Month-to-Date Summary
* Hourly Usage
* Top 500 Custom Metrics
